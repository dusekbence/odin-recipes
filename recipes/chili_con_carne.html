Chili con carne

Description

Chili con carne is originally a mexican dish but it has gone through many alterations. It's main ingredients are the beans, the meat and the chili, everything else is more or less optional.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bean
  • Corn
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make a meat sauce.
  2. Add spices.
  3. Add beans and corns.
  4. Eat with fresh bread.
