Burple nurples

Description

Burple nurples are a fictional dish from the 2000's cartoon Chowder from Cartoon Network. They are some kind of a cake with some kind of beautiful, funny plot point (like they are the ones who eat people, I dunno).

Ingredients

  • White cake mix
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Choco chips
  • Coriander
  • Purple food coloring

Steps

  1. Stir together the ingredients
  2. Add food coloring
  3. Pour in choco chips
  4. Grease a baking pan
  5. Pour the mixture into the baking pan and put in the oven
  6. Mold them into shape
